The Buganda Kingdom

Luwombo

Luwombo is a traditional Buganda dish where meat, chicken, or fish is steamed in banana leaves.

Buganda Palace

The Buganda Palace, known as Lubiri, is the official residence of the Kabaka, symbolizing the kingdom's cultural heritage.

Bakisimba

Buganda's cultural dance, known as Bakisimba, involves rhythmic drumming and wasit movements, celebrating the kingdom's traditions and unity.

His Highness The Kabaka Of Buganda

The Kabaka is the king of the Buganda Kingdom, serving as the central cultural and political leader.
